## Privacy Policy

The privacy of visitors to this site is important. This page summarizes what data is collected and how it is used.

### What is collected

Like most websites, this site may collect standard request metadata through hosting and analytics tooling, including:

* IP address
* Browser and device type
* Date/time of request
* Referrer and requested pages
* Aggregate traffic counts

This information is used in aggregate to understand site usage and improve content. It is not used to identify individual visitors.

### Analytics used on this site

This site currently uses GoatCounter for web analytics.

Google Analytics is not currently enabled.
Disqus-based commenting is not currently enabled.

### Log Files

Like many websites, log files may be used to monitor traffic patterns and basic site operations.

### Cookies and third-party services

This site does not require cookies for basic page access.
If you follow links to third-party services (for example publishers, social media, or video hosts), those services may set cookies or collect data under their own policies.

You can manage cookies and tracking behavior through your browser settings.
