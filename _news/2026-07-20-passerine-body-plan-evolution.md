Jacob Berv led "Rates of passerine body plan evolution in time and space" in *Nature Ecology & Evolution*, with graduate advisee Charlotte M. Probst as a co-author. [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41559-026-03110-5) · [Free access](https://rdcu.be/fuxB1)

<!--news-excerpt-->
Using more than 170,000 skeletal measurements across 2,057 passerine species, the study links rare bursts of body-plan innovation with climatic instability and present-day latitudinal patterns.
